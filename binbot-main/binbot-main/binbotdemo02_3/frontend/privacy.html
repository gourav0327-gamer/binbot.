Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Data Collection

At BinBot, your privacy is our priority. We only collect the information necessary to provide you with our intelligent waste classification service. This includes:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture (when signing in via Google).
  • Scan Data: Images and text queries you submit to the AI engine for the sole purpose of classification and rendering history logs.
  • Gamification Data: Your XP, levels, and ranks are stored locally on your device to create a tailored experience.

How We Use Your Data

We do not sell your data. period. The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the AI classification models (TensorFlow & Gemini).
  • Provide a personalized dashboard and analytics overview for your recycling habits.
  • Authenticate your secure login sessions using Firebase Authentication.

Image Privacy

When you use the Visual Scanner, the image analysis is performed predominantly on your local device via TensorFlow.js. We do not permanently store photos of your trash on our servers unless explicitly opted in for community training programs.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at binbot.ai@gmail.com.
